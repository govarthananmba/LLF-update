ROAD TO SCHOOL

Road to School addresses learning gaps among children in primary and middle grades and focusses on their holistic development, while Road to Livelihood equips senior students with minimum basic competencies to confidently navigate life after school

  • RTS Program, a flagship program
  • Aligned with the United Nations Global Sustainable Development Goals.
  • RTS program are multi-pronged to ensure holistic development
  • Critical thinking, sports activities, wellness sessions and community engagements.

Road to Livelihood

A series of targeted and structured interventions for the students in the last years of their schooling to ensure that they have the minimum basic competencies to confidently navigate life after school and guide them towards sustainable livelihoods for their future.

  • Career Counselling and Guidance: This vertical involves web-based psychometric tests for each student to map their interests and aptitude and subsequent career guidance based on the detailed report from the assessment by certified mentors.
  • Digital Literacy: Basic digital skills are indispensable in today’s world. Recognizing this, we have introduced digital literacy classes in a phased manner across Grades IX-XII to ensure that students are confident and comfortable using technology.
  • Spoken English: Many brilliant students often encounter obstacles due to their lack of conversational English; we seek to bridge that gap to make sure that a lack of spoken English does not pose as a hurdle to their potential and ambitions.
  • Adolescent Wellness: Adolescence is a tricky phase of life, and it requires considerable attention and guidance to make sure that young students make the best of the choices available to them. Currently, adolescent wellbeing sessions under RTL are conducted by volunteer Doctors who undertake awareness sessions for students on the topics of Substance Abuse and Menstrual Hygiene
